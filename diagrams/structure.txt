Laptop (Python/C++) ←→ USB ←→ Arduino (Nano/Uno) ←→ nRF24L01+ ←→ 2.4GHz Radio ←→ Drone Receiver
```

## Hardware Setup

### Components Needed
1. **Arduino Nano or Uno**
2. **nRF24L01+ module** (preferably with external antenna for better range)
3. **10μF capacitor** (across VCC/GND of nRF24L01+ for stability)
4. **USB cable** for Arduino-to-laptop connection
5. Optional: **nRF24L01+ adapter board** with voltage regulator

### nRF24L01+ Wiring to Arduino
```
nRF24L01+  →  Arduino Nano/Uno
--------------------------------
VCC        →  3.3V (IMPORTANT: NOT 5V!)
GND        →  GND
CE         →  Pin 9
CSN        →  Pin 10
SCK        →  Pin 13
MOSI       →  Pin 11
MISO       →  Pin 12
IRQ        →  Not connected (optional)
